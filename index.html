About Us

Who We Are

Team Pookies is a dynamic group of passionate tech enthusiasts who come together to create innovative solutions for real-world problems. Founded in 2024, our team has participated in multiple hackathons, securing top positions with our creative approaches and technical expertise.

Our Mission

We aim to leverage cutting-edge technology to develop sustainable and impactful solutions that address pressing challenges in today's digital landscape.

Our Vision

To become pioneers in technological innovation, fostering a community of problem-solvers dedicated to making a difference through code.
